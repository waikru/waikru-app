About Waikru

A second pair of eyes for your training

Waikru is an AI coaching tool built for Muay Thai practitioners who want honest, specific technique feedback between sessions. Upload a clip of your pad work, bag work, or shadow boxing and get a detailed report back in under two minutes.

The AI watches your full video and returns timestamped corrections tied to exactly what it sees happening in your footage. Not generic advice pulled from a template. Actual observations about your stance, your guard, your footwork, your combinations.

Think of it as a second pair of eyes when your coach isn't in the room. Something that catches the small things that are easy to miss and hard to self-correct without a reference point.

Waikru is in active development. The core analysis works well for pad work, bag work, and shadow boxing filmed from the side at waist height or above. Video quality and angle have a big effect on feedback quality — 720p from a phone propped against a bag is plenty.

Built in Lehigh Valley, PA. If you have feedback, thoughts, or you're a coach who wants to explore using this with your students, reach out at hello@waikru.app.
