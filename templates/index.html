Frequently asked questions about Waikru.

How Waikru handles your training video, what AI coaching feedback to expect, and what makes a good Muay Thai clip to upload.

What kind of video should I upload to Waikru?
A 1–3 minute clip of pad work, bag work, or shadow boxing works best. Film from the side with your whole body in frame; good lighting helps the AI see your technique.
What video file types and sizes does Waikru support?
Waikru supports MP4, MOV, WebM, AVI, and MKV up to 500 MB. 720p is plenty — higher resolutions slow the upload without improving feedback quality.
How long does the AI analysis take?
Usually under two minutes for a 3-minute clip. Upload time depends on your connection and file size. Live progress is shown while it runs.
Is my training video private on Waikru?
Your video is sent to Google Gemini for analysis and deleted from Waikru's servers immediately after the report is generated. Waikru does not store, share, or sell your footage.
How accurate is the AI Muay Thai feedback?
Gemini reliably catches stance, hand position, and obvious mechanical issues. Treat it as a second pair of eyes between sessions — not a replacement for a qualified coach.
Can I upload someone else's Muay Thai training video?
Only with their permission. Do not upload videos of minors without guardian consent or content you do not have the rights to share.
